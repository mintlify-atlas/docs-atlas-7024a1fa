# Welcome to Sovran

Sovran is a privacy-focused mobile Bitcoin wallet that brings the feel of physical cash to digital payments. Built on Cashu ecash protocol and powered by Nostr, Sovran enables instant Bitcoin transactions with near-zero fees while maintaining your privacy and sovereignty.

<img src="https://sovran.money" alt="Sovran Wallet" />

## What is Sovran?

Sovran combines cutting-edge Bitcoin technologies to create a seamless payment experience:

- **Cashu Ecash**: Send and receive Bitcoin instantly with blind signatures that protect your privacy
- **Lightning Network**: Lightning invoices and payments with BOLT11 support
- **Nostr Integration**: Decentralized messaging, contacts, and social features via Nostr protocol
- **NFC Payments**: Tap-to-pay functionality for contactless Bitcoin transactions
- **Multi-Mint Support**: Distribute your balance across multiple Cashu mints for security

## Core Features

### Bitcoin That Feels Like Cash

Ecash bearer tokens live on your device like digital cash. Send and receive instantly with near-zero fees. Blind signatures mean mints can't link your transactions.

### Powered by Nostr

Built on Nostr, a decentralized network that can't be shut down or censored. Your identity is derived from your seed using NIP-06, giving you a portable social identity tied to your wallet.

### Privacy by Design

- BIP-39 seed phrase generation and secure storage
- NIP-06 deterministic key derivation for Nostr identity
- Encrypted local storage via expo-secure-store
- No data collection — fully open source

### Multi-Mint Architecture

Choose mints you trust and spread your balance across many for redundancy. Every mint shows its KYM (Know Your Mint) score with audit data from the Cashu Auditor.

## Protocol Support

Sovran implements industry-standard Bitcoin and Nostr protocols:

**Cashu NUTs**: NUT-00 through NUT-13, NUT-17 (payment requests), NUT-18, NUT-23

**Lightning Network**: BOLT11 invoices for sending and receiving

**Nostr NIPs**: NIP-04, NIP-05, NIP-06, NIP-17 (gift-wrapped DMs), NIP-19, NIP-44, NIP-59

**Bitcoin Standards**: BIP-39 and BIP-32 for hierarchical deterministic wallets

## Why Sovran?

### Instant Payments

Send and receive Bitcoin instantly without waiting for Lightning channel management or blockchain confirmations. Ecash tokens settle immediately.

### Near-Zero Fees

Cashu ecash eliminates the routing fees and minimum payment amounts common with Lightning, making micropayments practical.

### Enhanced Privacy

Blind signatures ensure mints cannot link your sending and receiving transactions. Multi-mint distribution further protects your privacy.

### Open Source

Fully open source and transparent. No venture capital, no data collection, no tracking.
